Baberu no toshokan

Toshokan no subete no dansei to dōyō ni, watashi wa wakai koro ni tabi o shimashita. Watashi wa hon, osoraku mokuroku no katarogu o sagashite samayoimashita. Watashi no me wa watashi ga kaita mono o hotondo kaidoku dekinainode, watashi wa umareta rokkakkei kara wazuka sū rīgu de shinu junbi o shite imasu. Watashi ga shindara, tesuri kara watashi o nagetobasu keiken'na te ga tsukiru koto wa arimasen. Watashi no haka wa sokonashi no kūkidesu. Watashi no karada wa hateshinaku shizumi, hōkai shi, rakka ni yotte seisei sa reta kaze ni tokemasu. Sore wa mugendesu. Toshokan wa owari ga nai to iimasu. Risō shugi-sha wa, rokkakkei no heya wa zettai-tekina kūkan, matawa sukunakutomo watashitachi no kūkan no chokkan no hitsuyōna katachidearu to shuchō shite imasu. Karera wa, sankakkei ya gokakukei no heya wa kangae rarenai to kangaete imasu. (Jinpishugi-sha wa, karera no ekusutashī ga ōkina enkei no hon o fukumu enkei no heya o akiraka ni suru to shuchō shite iruga, sono sebone wa renzoku shite ori, kabe no kanzen'na en ni sotte iru. Shikashi, karera no shōgen wa utagawashī. Karera no kotoba wa aimaidearu. ) Koko de, koten-tekina kakugen o kurikaesu dakede jūbundesu. Toshokan wa, seikakuna chūshin ga rokkakkei no izurekadeari, sono enshū ni wa akusesudekinai-kyūdesu.
